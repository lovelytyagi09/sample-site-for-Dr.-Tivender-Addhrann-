Warning Signs

Signs You May Have an Allergy

Many allergy symptoms are mistaken for a common cold or skin condition. If these sound familiar, it is time to consult Dr. Tivender Addhrann.

Respiratory & General Symptoms

  • Frequent or unexplained sneezing
  • Persistent runny or blocked nose
  • Itchy or watery eyes
  • Coughing, wheezing, or breathlessness
  • Recurring sore throat or postnasal drip
  • Fatigue or brain fog following exposure to triggers
  • Swelling of the lips, tongue, or throat

Skin & Gut Symptoms

  • Red, itchy, or inflamed patches on skin
  • Hives or raised welts appearing suddenly
  • Dry, flaky, or cracked skin (eczema)
  • Rash after eating certain foods or touching surfaces
  • Stomach cramps, bloating, or diarrhoea after food
  • Nausea or vomiting after meals
  • Recurring skin reactions with no clear cause
