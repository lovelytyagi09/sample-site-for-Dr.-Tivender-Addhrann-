About the Clinic

A Trusted Clinical Home for Delhi Families

Trisha Family Clinic was established in 2014 by Dr. Tivender Addhrann — bringing together decades of AIIMS-trained clinical experience, public health expertise, and genuine compassion for every patient who walks through the door.

Family-Centred Care

One trusted doctor for every member of your family — newborns to seniors — under one roof.

Evidence-Based Medicine

Treatments backed by 30+ national & international research projects. No guesswork — ever.

Same-Day Consultations

Walk-in or pre-booked appointments available. Evening and weekend OPD for working families.

Home Visits Available

Can't make it in? Dr. Addhrann offers home visits for follow-ups and non-emergency care. Home visits are possible in extreme conditions due to unavailability of time.
