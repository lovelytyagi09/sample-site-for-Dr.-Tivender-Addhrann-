About This Service

Complete Child Health Care — Every Stage, Every Need

Children are not small adults — they require specialised clinical attention at each stage of development. Dr. Tivender Addhrann's child health consultations are tailored to your child's age, stage, and specific health needs, ensuring they grow up healthy, strong, and well-cared for.

Comprehensive assessment of your child's growth, nutrition, and development
Diagnosis and management of common and complex childhood illnesses
Complete immunisation schedules — from birth through adolescence
Monitoring of developmental milestones and early intervention when needed
Guidance for parents on nutrition, sleep, behaviour, and healthy habits
Adolescent health — puberty, mental wellness, and lifestyle counselling

Care for Every Age

Tailored Care at Every Stage of Childhood

From the first days of life through the teenage years, each stage of childhood brings unique health needs — and we are equipped for all of them.

0 – 1 Year

Newborn & Infant

Newborn health assessments, feeding guidance, weight gain monitoring, jaundice checks, and early vaccination schedules.

1 – 6 Years

Toddler & Pre-School

Growth milestones, speech and motor development, nutrition, immunity-building, and management of frequent infections and fevers.

6 – 12 Years

School Age

Academic performance concerns, attention and behaviour, allergy management, dental health awareness, sports fitness, and annual checkups.

13 – 18 Years

Adolescent

Puberty guidance, mental and emotional wellness, weight management, lifestyle counselling, and adolescent-specific health concerns.

What We Treat & Offer

Comprehensive Child Health Services at Our Clinic

Everything your child needs — under one trusted roof, with one doctor who knows their full health history.

Growth & Development Monitoring

Regular tracking of height, weight, head circumference, and developmental milestones to ensure your child is thriving on schedule.

Vaccination & Immunisation

Complete IAP and government immunisation schedules — from BCG at birth through all recommended childhood and adolescent vaccines.

Fever, Cold & Infections

Accurate diagnosis and treatment of fever, viral infections, throat infections, ear infections, and recurrent respiratory illnesses.

Asthma & Breathing Concerns

Assessment and long-term management of childhood asthma, wheezing, and breathing difficulties with personalised action plans.

Developmental & Behavioural

Evaluation of speech delay, attention difficulties, learning concerns, and behavioural issues with appropriate guidance and referrals.

Child Nutrition Guidance

Tailored dietary advice for each age group — addressing undernutrition, picky eating, obesity, iron deficiency, and healthy eating habits.

When to Visit

Signs Your Child Needs a Consultation Today

Don't wait — these symptoms in children warrant prompt medical attention from an experienced physician.

High or persistent fever
Difficulty breathing
Frequent vomiting or diarrhoea
Poor weight gain or loss
Skin rash or allergies
Speech or hearing concerns
Delayed developmental milestones
Recurrent ear or throat infections
Behavioural or learning issues
Vaccination due / overdue
Abdominal pain or constipation
Routine annual checkup
